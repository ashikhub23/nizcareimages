1. OSHWC Compliance Made Simple
As per Occupational Safety, Health & Working Conditions (OSHWC) code guidelines, construction workers—especially those above 40 years of age—must undergo periodic health check-ups to identify health risks early and prevent workplace incidents. Preventive healthcare measures and maintaining employee health records are mandatory under the OSHWC Code.
 
 
 
3. Safety Through Partnership
Together, OSHWC compliance and Nizcare services help create safer sites, healthier workers, and uninterrupted projects. Regular health check-ups protect workers for life, ensuring preventive healthcare and mandatory health records are maintained for construction workers, especially those above 40 years of age. A healthy worker is truly a safe worker - Nizcare + OSHWC equals Safer Work, Healthier Life.
