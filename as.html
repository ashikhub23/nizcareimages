In the manufacturing industry, employee health and safety are critical due to shop-floor exposure, physical workload, shift duties, and operational risks. These challenges require constant vigilance and proactive safety measures to protect workers and maintain productivity.

Our solutions help you navigate these complex safety requirements while optimizing your operations.

OSHWC Framework Compliance

Under the OSHWC framework, organizations are required to implement preventive health measures and maintain employee health records as part of statutory compliance. This includes regular assessments, documentation, and continuous improvement processes.

We provide comprehensive tools and expertise to ensure your organization meets all regulatory requirements efficiently.

Periodic health check-ups for employees aged 40 years and above are mandatory, helping organizations identify risks early and ensure workplace safety. Our preventive care programs go beyond compliance to promote long-term employee wellness and productivity.
